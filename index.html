A New Philosophy of Data Analysis

Discover the Questions
Hidden in Your Data

In the landscape of data, most tools are designed to excavate answers. But in a world where answers are abundant, we've optimized for retrieval, not revelation, leaving us to search only for what we already know to look for.

The most profound insights, however, are not found in better answers but are buried beneath the questions we haven't yet learned how to ask. True data analysis is not the mechanical extraction of predetermined truths, but a partnership in discovery guided by curiosity and wonder.

Join the Waitlist

BambooAI is an experimental tool that makes data analysis more accessible by allowing users to interact with their data through natural language conversations. It's designed to process natural language queries, generate and execute Python code for analysis and visualization, and help users derive insights without extensive coding knowledge.

As a user, you will get a fully private, sandboxed 16GB RAM environment to work with your data. Due to our limited processing capacity, we are introducing a waitlist. Please note that while the service is free, you will be required to provide your own API keys for the AI vendors/models you wish to use.
